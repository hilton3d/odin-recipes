Ice

Description

Ice is frozen water. It's often frozen into cubes used to keep drinks cold. Other liquids can be frozen as well, but water's the most common substance applied.

Ingredients

  • water
  • a freezer

Steps

  1. Go to grocery store
  2. Purchase bag of ice
  3. Put ice bag in freezer
  4. When ready, remove ice bag from freezer and remove the amount of ice cubes desired
  5. Put ice cubs into drinking cup or glass
